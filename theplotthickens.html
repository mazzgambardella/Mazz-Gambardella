THE PLOT THICKENS


In the summer of 2018 I was asked to do branding and artwork designs for a new podcast called ‘ The Plot Thickens’. A comedy podcast which revolves around creating plots for new movies, the podcasters wanted to make sure the branding revealed a light hearted side and also the heavy influence on movies of the golden age of Hollywood, these are the results of this.
